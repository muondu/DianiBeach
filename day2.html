Morning

The next day We woke up,wore our swimming costume,prepared our self and then we went to the swimming.Our Father told as our cousin Kingori is comming.We were so happy.Kingori came with his father.

Afternoon

We played together until lunch time we went to eat lunch.After that we played until it reaches 4 oclock,we go to drink coco and then we went to the ocean.At6 oclock,we got out of the ocean to go to the room.

Evening

We went to shower, changed and went to eat supper.After supper we went to Coco Jambo.We played and then we went to the room to sleep.
