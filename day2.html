Morning

The next day,we woke up prepaired our self and then we wore our swimming costume to go to the ocean.We found it was low tid.A man came and took us to the ocean.

We saw many animals hidding under rocks. One of the animals we saw first was called sea acher.The femail ones were brown and the male ones are black.A nother animal we saw was called a stone fish.It blends with the rocks because it has the same colour with the rocks.That's how it got its name.The stone fish has a poison on there scales.Another animal we saw was a sea animal.Its protectishon is its gum.Sea animal has steaky gum which is poisonus to other animals exsept from Humans.We came back from the ocean and we went to the swimming pool.

Afternoon

After 4 oclock.Then we went to the ocean.After we went to the ocean,we went to the swimming pool.We ate lunch whent to the swimming.At 4 oclock,we went to drink cocoe.After that we went to the ocean the waves were big.

Evening

After the ocean we went to shower change and went to eat supper.After supper I went with my small cousin to Coco Jambo.Coco Jambo is the room were you can play.We played and then my brother and my big cousin came to play too.Then my father came to play with me pull.We played antil the place was closed.We went back home to sleep.
