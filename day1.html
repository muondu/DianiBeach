Morning

It was a sunny morning.I prepared my self to go to Mombasa with my father,Brother Malli and my cousin Sharon.My Father called a taxi to drop us at Jomo Kenyata International Airport.When we reached the Airport,we saw many aeroplanes in the Airport.We bought our tickets and went to the Aeroplane.The Aeroplane was called Jambo Jet.

Then we took of.The plane was very comfortable.Then I felt hungry.Lukily my mother had brought some fruits.I ate some bananas and an apple.We landed after 48 minutes.Then my Father called another taxi to come to pick us from the Airport to the hotel.

Afternoon

After we reached the hotel, we waited for our key to the room.Then I and found the beach and the swimming pool.I was happyy as a king.I went to tell my father.In a drop of a hat, we wore our swimming costume and then we went to the swimming pool.

Then we went to the ocean.In the ocean some waves were small while others were huge.We like the big ones becoause we went underwater and the wave would push you rearward to the shore.

In the Afternoon you can it lunch at lunch time,playing or swimming inthe sswimming pool .You can go to play in the beach and relax.Many children play in the swimming pool.When it reaches 4 oclock.You can go to the ocean because it was starting to become high tide.There was also a watching point were you could enjoy watching the waves or go to the waves.You can stay in the swimming pool or go to have a snack.

Evening

When we reached home we were shocked to see our bags were there.Our Dad told us the people of the hotel took the bags in the room.We went to shower,change and went to eat supper.There were so many things to choose from.I choose so many things.When everyone was full, we all went home to watch TV.
